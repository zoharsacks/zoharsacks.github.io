Enterprise Software Israel Meetup

May 08, 2015

The Israeli tech scene consist of several global market leaders such as Amdocs, Checkpoint, NICE and many more, a direct derivative is a lot of Israeli enterprise software knowledge.

Ther are great benefits in sharing knowledge both for the sharer and the sharee, but there where no “knowledge sharing space” and most knowladge sharing was based on personal relations so I’ve decided to scale the knowledge sharing and founded Enterprise Software Israel Meetup.

In each meetup session we put in the same room startups and well established companies and assist them cross pollinate, we encourage open discussion and networking and had placed the cornerstone for several nice collaborations.

We see valuable content as the king in “Enterprise Software Israel Meetup”, therefor we have “the three rules of Enterprise Software Israel”

—The Rules of Enterprise Software Israel—

  1. You do talk about Enterprise Software Israel.
  2. You do talk about Enterprise Software Israel.
  3. Content is the king - only A grade speakers who speak to the point and happy to have discussion are invited.
  4. Strict advertisement free policy, commercial entities that wishes to communicate with the community using the meetup must bring value to the table (sponsorship, special promo code, meetup swags and perks etc.).

if you like to hear more about my vision, give a hand, suggest topic, give a talk or sponsor a session ping me.
