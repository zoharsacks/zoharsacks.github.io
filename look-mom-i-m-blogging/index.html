Look Mom, I'm Blogging

Apr 28, 2015


It has been about 15 years since I first wanted to blog.
15 long years of going from “I have so much to say” to “but nobody gives a sh!t” until one day I had the luck to work for the amazing Arnon Rotem-Gal-Oz which blogs mainly about technology he uses. I had hours of conversations with Arnon about technology, bringing value, Bigdata, software architecture and my wish to blog…

Arnon solved my conflicts quite easily, “if you want to blog, just blog!”, The Plain and Simple Solution to Most of Our Creativity Barriers.
At this point I got into the very fun procrastination game of “selecting the blogging platform” this fullish game can kill every initiative very fast, you focus on the wrong issues and wast your creative energy on solving them…

To cut a long story short - I’ve selected the easiest blogging system, customized it as little as possible and now I’m letting the content do it’s thing, if this blog will give any value to anyone I’ll have plenty of time to fiddle with it.

I’ll use this blog to share my view of my topics of interest, of which I can count:

  • Technology
  • Entrepreneurship and Startups
  • Product
  • People
  • Management
  • Improvisation Theater and my amazing group Shirley and the Cans
  • My meetup Enterprise Software Israel Meetup

Hope you will love my writing, will be happy to hear your thoughts, all my contact forms are on the left sidebar here please drop me a note.

Look mom, I’m blogging
